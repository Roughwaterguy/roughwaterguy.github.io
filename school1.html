Indeed at the end of journal 1 their was a link.
Links are fairly simple to make, all you need is the <a>
tag. Inside of it is a attribute called a href, it looks
something like this <a href="website.link">Click me</a>.

Now lets make a list of links,

  • Videos
  • Games
  • Images

With this you could make a top ten list
or maybe a list of youtube videos in a series.
But put that all aside because we don't even
have a way to represent data. You know what'd be
good for that? A table!
